About lostelectro

Lostelectro is an open project dedicated to dwelling and publishing rare music with no copyrights being active.
Our mission is to filter which music is completely lost - and could be freely published - and those, which copyrights must be updated.


If you want to claim any copyrights about music on lostelectro project, or if you have any questions - you are welcome
